üîç How to Identify Geodes in the Field

Weight Test
Hollow geodes feel lighter than solid rocks of the same size
Sound Test
Tap with a rock - hollow geodes produce a different sound
Shape
Look for round to oval, nodular shapes
Surface
Often weathered, bumpy exterior, sometimes with crystal exposure

‚ò¢Ô∏è Safety Guidelines for Radioactive Specimens

Storage Requirements
Lead-lined containers, separate from living areas, proper ventilation
Handling Precautions
Minimal contact time, wash hands thoroughly, use tweezers/tongs
Monitoring Equipment
Geiger counter essential, dosimeter badges recommended for serious collectors
Legal Considerations
Check local regulations - some areas limit possession of radioactive materials
